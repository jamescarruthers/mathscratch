## [0.0.1] - 2025-01-15

### Added

- Initial release
- Real-time inline mathematical expression evaluation
- Auto-completion for expressions ending with `=`
- Variable support with scope tracking
- Configurable result alignment
- Custom formatting for binary, hexadecimal, and octal numbers
- Unit support via mathjs
